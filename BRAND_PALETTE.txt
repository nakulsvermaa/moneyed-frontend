moneyed - Brand Palette & Colorways
====================================
Symbol: a CLIMBING TURTLE (ascending) with a check badge - steady, trusted,
upward financial growth. ("Your Money . Your Future")

CORE COLORS
  Deep Green     #085C3A   rgb(8, 92, 58)      Primary brand
  Bright Green   #18C979   rgb(24, 201, 121)   Accent / bright tile / check badge
  Mint Green     #A9E7CE   rgb(169, 231, 206)  Fresh accent
  Mint BG        #F0FFFA   rgb(240, 255, 250)  Fresh background
  Warm Yellow    #E9E4A0   rgb(233, 228, 160)  Earthy accent
  Cream BG       #FFFDF0   rgb(255, 253, 240)  Earthy background
  Muted Green    #2E7B4F   rgb(46, 123, 79)    Earthy tile
  White          #FFFFFF                        Clean

COLORWAYS (4)
  1. DEEP GREEN . PRIMARY   deep-green bg, bright-green tile, white wordmark
  2. MINT GREEN . FRESH     mint bg, deep-green tile, deep wordmark
  3. WHITE . CLEAN          white bg, deep-green tile, deep wordmark
  4. WARM YELLOW . EARTHY   cream bg, muted-green tile (yellow badge), deep wordmark

TYPEFACE
  Poppins (Bold = "moneyed", SemiBold = tagline). Outlined to vector paths in
  all SVGs, so it renders identically without the font installed.
