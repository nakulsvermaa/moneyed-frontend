Fin-Shorts — Daily Money Tips

Pro Tip 1 min read

Your CIBIL score drops by 7-10 points for every loan inquiry!

When multiple banks check your score simultaneously, it signals desperation to lenders. Apply to one lender at a time to protect your score.

Myth Busted 1 min read

Closing an old credit card does NOT improve your score!

It actually reduces your "credit age" and "available limit" — both of which lower your score. Keep old cards active with small purchases.

Did You Know? 1 min read

Indians pay ₹1.2 Lakh crore extra annually in unnecessary interest!

Simply shifting a Personal Loan from 18% to 11% on ₹5L can save you ₹1.8 Lakh over 5 years. That's enough for a used car!
